# Dr. Marc B. Nock | Physician-Scientist

### **Computational Neurology | Systems Biology | Public Health Strategy**

I am a researcher focused on the intersection of biological hardware and digital information systems. My work centers on the **Nock Framework**, a bio-computational approach to identifying systemic fatigue and neurodegeneration through the analysis of the **Neural Echo**.

---

### **Core Research Areas**
* **Trigeminal Gateway Theory:** Mapping the craniofacial-neural interface as a high-bandwidth data port.
* **Bio-Computational Latency:** Quantifying the thermodynamic cost of neural entropy and structural static.
* **Federated Learning:** Developing privacy-preserving models for population-scale public health data.
